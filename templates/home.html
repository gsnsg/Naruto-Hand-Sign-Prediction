About:

Used CNN to predict naruto hand sign. The dataset can be found here.
I used all the images from train and test set and shuffled them before splitting them into respective sets. I used a test split of 0.2 and an validation split of 0.2 and batch size = 32 for 20 epochs.
The validation accuracy at the end of training was ~95% and the test accuracy was ~96%
